MTJJ(born 27 November 1984)

He is an independent animation producer from China, the director of The Legend of Hei


Experience


1. In high school because MTJJ was relatively dull, his classmates named Mu Tou(means dull in chinese), so he took a net name called “Mu Tou”, but because Mu Tou had a higher repetition rate, he added “JJ”

2. MTJJ originally studied art design, and later joined an animation company, so he embarked on the path of no return to animation production.

3. MTJJ once adopted a stray cat called "XiaoHei", and later the animation "The Legend of Hei" was born.

4. On March 17, 2011, the Flash animation "The Legend of Hei" produced by MTJJ and his studio was released.

5. On September 7, 2019, the movie "The Legend of Hei" created by MTJJ and his studio was screened in theatres nationwide.
